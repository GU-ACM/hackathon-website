3rd Place

Sip Safe

Sip Safe is an app designed to help Gonzaga students consume alcohol safely and advises them on when they should stop, when they should seek medical attention, and allows them to enjoy themselves responsibly. Sip Safe requires a Gonzaga student email to authenticate and takes in your sex, height, weight, and consumption to give a rough estimate of your blood alcohol level over a certain time period.
