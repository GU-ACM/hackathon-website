    About

    Have an idea for a project you've never had the time to actually put together?

    Want to learn a new technology? Looking for some resume builders?

    Register for the Hackathon and see what you can hack together in 12 hours!

    Hackathon Schedule

    8:00 am - Check-in opens

    9:30 am - Keynote speaker

    10:00 am - Hacking begins

    12:00 pm - Lunch

    6:00 pm - Dinner

    10:00 pm - Judging begins

    11:30 pm - Judging ends

    11:30 pm - Awards Ceremony

    Ground Rules

    Do not hack ahead of time. You are allowed to plan beforehand, but no coding.


    Do not sabotage or harm other teams, unless given express permission. This is a friendly competition.


    Bring your own gear, including power strips and chargers. We may not have enough spare for everyone.


    You are allowed to leave at any time. You are not required to stay until the end for judging.

    FAQs

    Do I have to go to Gonzaga to participate?

    Students from other colleges and nearby high schools can also participate.


    If I sign up, do I need to stay for the whole competition?

    You are allowed to leave whenever. If you stay to the end you do not need to submit your project for judging.


    Will there be food?

    Yes food will be provided. Let us know of any allergies and we will do our best to accomidate.


    Is it free? Do I need a team?

    Yup, our hackathon is completely free. You can participate solo or with a team. We can also assign you to a team if you want.
